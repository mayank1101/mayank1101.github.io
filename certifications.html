Why I Keep Learning

The AI landscape moves fast — what's state-of-the-art today is a baseline tomorrow. For an AI product professional, that means two things: staying technically fluent enough to make sound architecture and evaluation decisions, and building product intuition that keeps user needs at the center regardless of how the technology shifts.

These certifications represent deliberate learning across both dimensions:

  • Technical Depth: Understanding the AI systems I'm designing and evaluating — not just the interfaces
  • Product Craft: Frameworks for roadmapping, prioritization, experimentation, and measuring impact
  • Evaluation Rigour: Building the right metrics and feedback loops, not just shipping features
  • Industry Standards: Learning from leading organizations on how to govern, deploy, and iterate on AI responsibly
  • Validated Credibility: Demonstrating expertise that complements real-world project delivery

Certifications validate structured knowledge — but real expertise shows up in shipped products. See how I apply these skills in my blog, or read about the production systems I've built on my about page.
