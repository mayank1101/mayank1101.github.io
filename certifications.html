Why I Keep Learning

The AI landscape moves fast. What requires a PhD today is a library call tomorrow. Keeping up means two things: going deep on the fundamentals that don't change, and staying current on the tools and techniques that do.

These certifications represent deliberate learning across both dimensions:

  • Technical Depth: Understanding AI systems at the level needed to build and debug them in production
  • Evaluation Rigor: Building the right metrics and feedback loops for trustworthy AI
  • Emerging Techniques: Staying current on LLMs, RAG, fine-tuning, and model optimization
  • Data Discipline: Using data rigorously — honest about what it shows, not just what looks good
  • Validated Expertise: Credentials that complement and confirm real-world delivery

Certifications validate structured knowledge, but real expertise shows up in production systems. Read how I think about AI in Token By Token, or see the systems I've built and the outcomes they achieved on my about page.
