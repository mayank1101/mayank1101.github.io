What These Show

Users First

The best features in both products came directly from user conversations — not internal assumptions. Citations in KnowBot. Audio playback in DataPulse. Both emerged from listening, not guessing.

Metrics That Matter

Setting the evaluation bar — what "trustworthy enough to ship" means — is a product decision. I define quality criteria before engineers build, not after users complain.

POCs Before Commitment

Before asking engineers to build anything significant, I prototype to validate. A lightweight POC that proves or disproves an idea saves weeks of misdirected engineering.

Phased Delivery

Both products shipped in phases. Phase I validated the core idea. Phase II extended it based on real feedback. Shipping incrementally builds trust and keeps direction grounded in evidence.
