What These Show

Discovery Before Building

Every case study starts with the same question: what is the actual problem? Stakeholder interviews, user conversations, and competitive analysis — before a single line of code.

PoC Before Commitment

Before committing engineering effort, I validate with lightweight prototypes. A focused PoC that proves or disproves an approach saves weeks of misdirected work.

Metrics That Matter

I define what "good enough to ship" means before engineers build, not after users complain. Groundedness, MRR, retrieval time, effort reduction: chosen deliberately, not retroactively.

End-to-End Ownership

From problem definition to production delivery — I stay accountable across the full arc. Stakeholder alignment, architecture decisions, evaluation, and coordinated shipping.

Let's Talk Product
