My Story in Applied AI

My M.Tech in Data Science at IIT Jammu taught me to think rigorously about evidence — what the data actually shows, not what we wish it showed. That discipline has shaped everything that followed.

At nference.ai, I built production AI systems for healthcare: NLP pipelines using BioClinicalBERT and SciBERT, knowledge distillation that reduced model size by 8× while achieving 9× inference speedup, and multimodal AI combining chest X-ray imaging with clinical text. I co-authored OpenMedLM (AAAI 2024, NEJM AI), the first open-source model to surpass 80% accuracy on the MMLU medical-subset using prompt engineering, not fine-tuning.

After nference, I worked across a range of AI engineering roles — independent consulting (Forta Health, AISECT), pharmaceutical intelligence at NeurIOT Labs, and LLM evaluation for smart TV platforms at LG Soft India. Each expanded my experience across domains: healthcare, pharma, consumer AI.

At Lamipak, I owned two AI products end-to-end: LamiOps (Enterprise AI Assistant - Operations, 10K+ daily users) and LamiTracker (Regulatory Intelligence Platform - R&D, 200+ sources, 18+ countries, 95% reduction in manual R&D tracking). I ran the full pipeline — stakeholder discovery, PoC experimentation, system architecture, evaluation framework, coordinated delivery.

I write Token by Token to share how I think about AI systems and the products built on top of them.
