About Me

I'm a product-oriented AI professional with 5+ years of experience delivering high-impact Generative AI solutions that drive measurable business value. I translate ambiguous business problems into production-grade AI systems — leading end-to-end product lifecycle from ideation and MVP development to deployment, evaluation, and iteration. My work spans Generative AI, LLMs, multimodal RAG, and enterprise AI consulting across manufacturing, healthcare, and technology domains.

M.Tech CSE - Data Science, IIT Jammu · India

My Story

My journey in AI began during my M.Tech in Data Science at IIT Jammu, where I developed a deep fascination for how machines can understand and generate human language. What started as academic curiosity quickly grew into something bigger — a drive to build AI that creates real, measurable value for users and businesses.

At nference.ai, I learned that the hardest problem in AI isn't the model — it's the product. Shipping reliable clinical NLP systems that healthcare professionals actually trust taught me to think deeply about user needs, evaluation rigor, and the gap between research accuracy and production reliability. This is where I developed my product instincts.

Today, I work as an AI Consultant and product owner, leading end-to-end development of Generative AI products for global enterprise clients. I define the roadmap, collaborate with cross-functional stakeholders, establish success metrics, and own delivery — from the first problem statement to the system serving users at scale. Every engagement starts with the business problem, not the technology.

Beyond my client work, I write Token by Token — a blog on AI, LLMs, RAG, and the practical craft of building GenAI products. I believe that understanding the systems beneath the tools is what separates great AI PMs from the rest.
