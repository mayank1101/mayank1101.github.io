About Me

I'm a product owner and data scientist making a deliberate move into Product Management. For 4+ years, I've worked at the intersection of user problems and technical solutions — talking to customers and departments, identifying problems worth solving, defining roadmaps, and coordinating with engineering teams to deliver. My background in data science shapes how I approach decisions: with care about evidence, honest trade-offs, and a focus on what users actually need.

M.Tech CSE - Data Science, IIT Jammu · India

My Story: The Transition to Product

My journey began with a fundamental belief: the best solutions are built on understanding users and data. During my M.Tech in Data Science at IIT Jammu, I learned to think analytically about problems — but I quickly realized that technology alone doesn't drive impact. It's how you apply it to solve real user problems that matters.

At nference.ai, I worked alongside teams building products that healthcare professionals actually used and trusted. I learned that technical impressiveness means nothing if users don't want it. This is where I started thinking more carefully about product decisions — what matters, how to measure it, and whether what we were building actually served user needs.

At Lamipak, I owned LamiOps and LamiTracker as product owner. LamiOps served 10K+ daily users. LamiTracker grew to span 200+ sources across 18+ countries. I talked to customers, identified problems, proposed solutions, designed roadmaps, aligned stakeholders, and worked closely with AI engineers, backend and frontend developers to deliver. That experience made something clear: what I find most meaningful is figuring out what to build and why — and working with teams to make it real.

Today, I'm making a deliberate move into Product Management. My analytics background shapes how I approach decisions — I care about measuring the right things and grounding choices in evidence. I write Token by Token to share how I think about building products that matter.
