My Story: From Building AI to Leading It

My M.Tech in Data Science at IIT Jammu taught me to think rigorously about evidence — what the data actually shows, not what we wish it showed. That discipline shaped everything that followed.

At nference.ai, I built production AI systems for healthcare: NLP pipelines using BioClinicalBERT and SciBERT, knowledge distillation that reduced model size by 8× while preserving performance, and multimodal AI combining medical imaging and clinical text. What I learned there: technical impressiveness means nothing if users don't want it. That's when I started thinking more carefully about product decisions.

At Lamipak, I owned LamiOps and LamiTracker as product owner. LamiOps served 10K+ daily users — a RAG-powered enterprise assistant I took from product discovery through PoC experimentation to full AI pipeline development and quality evaluation. LamiTracker spanned 200+ sources across 18+ countries — I designed and built the complete AI and backend system for it. Across both, I talked to customers, identified problems, defined roadmaps, and coordinated with the frontend developer to deliver. That experience made one thing clear: deciding what to build and why is the work I find most meaningful.

Today, I'm completing a Product Management Fellowship at NextLeap to bring structure to what I've already been doing in practice.
