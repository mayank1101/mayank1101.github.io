My Story

My journey began with a fundamental belief: the best solutions are built on understanding users and data. During my M.Tech in Data Science at IIT Jammu, I learned how to think analytically about problems — but I quickly realized that technology alone doesn't drive impact. It's how you apply it to solve real user problems that matters.

At nference.ai, I gained deep technical expertise while learning a critical lesson: shipping products that clinicians trust requires more than optimization metrics. It requires obsessive focus on reliability, user needs, and evaluation rigor. I optimized models for production constraints, championed evaluation frameworks, and learned that technical excellence is a means to solving user problems — not an end in itself.

At Lamipak, I made a deliberate shift toward product thinking. I owned LamiOps (operational analytics platform serving 10K+ users) and LamiTracker (regulatory intelligence across 200+ sources, 18+ countries). I defined product strategy, aligned stakeholders, measured success with rigorous KPIs, and understood that product leadership is about translating ambiguous problems into solutions that users love and businesses need.

Today, I position myself as a Product Manager with uncommon technical depth. My background in data science and analytics is not just a credential — it's a competitive advantage. I can collaborate deeply with engineering and data teams, make smarter product decisions grounded in data, and bridge the gap between technical possibility and user value. Beyond client work, I write Token by Token — technical deep-dives on building products that matter.
