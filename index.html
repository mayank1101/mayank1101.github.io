Token by Token

How AI systems actually work — no CS degree required, no depth sacrificed.

Most explanations of AI go one of two ways.

They either drown you in equations and jargon, or they hand you an analogy so shallow it answers nothing.

Token by Token is the middle path. Written for people who are genuinely curious about how AI works, what product teams actually do with it, and why any of it matters, without needing to write a single line of code to follow along.

No jargon walls. No hand-wavy oversimplifications.

Each piece starts with the question a curious person would actually ask, then follows it honestly, as far as the real answer goes. When a concept needs a bit of depth to make sense, we go there. When it doesn't, we don't.

The goal isn't to make AI feel simple. It's to make it feel understandable.
